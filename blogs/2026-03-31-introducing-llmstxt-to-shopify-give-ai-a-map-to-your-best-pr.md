<p>You&#8217;ve&nbsp;worked hard to build your product catalog. The last thing you want is AI tools like ChatGPT or Google Gemini describing your products inaccurately to potential customers.&nbsp;</p>



<p>AI tools&nbsp;don&#8217;t&nbsp;browse your whole store the way a search engine does. They grab what they can&nbsp;find,&nbsp;quickly, and fill in the gaps. For a store with a large catalog, that means incomplete answers, outdated information, or worse, sending shoppers to a competitor.&nbsp;</p>



<p>The new&nbsp;<a href="https://yoast.com/features/llms-txt/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">llms.txt feature</a>, available&nbsp;in Yoast SEO for Shopify&nbsp;bridges that gap.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-what-does-it-actually-do-nbsp">What does it actually do?&nbsp;</h2>



<p>It creates a file that tells AI tools which parts of your store matter most: your top products, your collections, your policies, and your key pages. Think of it as handing AI a well-organized store guide instead of letting it wander around on its own.&nbsp;</p>



<p>You switch it&nbsp;on&nbsp;once. We handle the rest.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-two-ways-to-use-it-nbsp">Two ways to use it&nbsp;</h2>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-let-yoast-handle-it-automatically-nbsp">Let Yoast handle it automatically&nbsp;</h3>



<p>Turn it on and&nbsp;we&#8217;ll&nbsp;build and update the file&nbsp;each week&nbsp;based on your Shopify data. No decisions&nbsp;needed. The file automatically highlights:&nbsp;</p>



<ul class="wp-block-list">
<li>Your 10 most-sold products over time</li>



<li>Up to 5 of your largest collections, plus a link to your full product range </li>



<li>Your store policies, including shipping, returns, and privacy </li>



<li>Your homepage, latest blog posts, and most recently updated pages </li>



<li>Any pages you&#8217;ve already marked as cornerstone content </li>
</ul>



<h3 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-or-choose-exactly-what-s-included-nbsp">Or choose exactly what&#8217;s included&nbsp;</h3>



<p>If&nbsp;you&#8217;d&nbsp;rather have full control, switch to manual&nbsp;selection. You can hand-pick the products and pages you want to feature, and&nbsp;there&#8217;s&nbsp;a dedicated spot to add your &#8220;About us&#8221;&nbsp;page&nbsp;so AI knows the story behind your brand.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Either way, the file updates&nbsp;weekly&nbsp;and removes deleted products automatically.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-no-technical-knowledge-needed">No technical knowledge needed</h2>



<p>Setting this up from scratch would normally mean editing code.&nbsp;We&#8217;ve&nbsp;built it directly into your Yoast SEO&nbsp;for Shopify&nbsp;settings so any member of your team can turn it on in seconds. If you already have a redirect set up for /llms.txt,&nbsp;we&#8217;ll&nbsp;respect it and let you know, so nothing breaks.&nbsp;</p>



<h2 class="wp-block-heading" id="h-you-decide-when-nbsp-it-s-nbsp-right-for-your-business-nbsp">You decide when&nbsp;it&#8217;s&nbsp;right for your business&nbsp;</h2>



<p>We believe every merchant should have a say in how their content is seen and used as AI plays a bigger role in how people discover products online.&nbsp;That&#8217;s&nbsp;why this feature is opt-in.&nbsp;</p>



<p>Turn on the llms.txt toggle in Yoast SEO for Shopify&nbsp;next time you&nbsp;<a href="https://admin.shopify.com/" rel="noreferrer noopener" target="_blank">log in&nbsp;to your store</a>.&nbsp;</p>
